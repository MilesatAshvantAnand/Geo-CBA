Low wind shear

This is a situation in which the wind speed or direction changes little with altitude. Low wind shear is favorable for the development and intensification of tropical cyclones.

Warm ocean waters

This refers to ocean waters with a temperature of at least 26.5 degrees Celsius (79.7 degrees Fahrenheit). Warm ocean waters provide tropical cyclones with the energy they need to develop and intensify.
